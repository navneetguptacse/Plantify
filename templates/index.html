What is the best season to grow rice in India?

Rice is typically grown in India during the rainy season, which lasts from June to September. This is because rice requires a lot of water to grow, and the rainy season provides the necessary moisture.

What is the most common disease for tomato in India, and what is the solution?

Tomatoes in India are often affected by diseases like early blight, late blight, and bacterial wilt. The solution to these diseases involves crop rotation, use of disease-resistant varieties, proper irrigation and drainage, and spraying fungicides or bactericides as necessary.

What is the best season to grow cotton in India?

Cotton is generally grown in India during the summer season, which lasts from March to June. This is because cotton requires a hot and dry climate to grow.

What is the best fertilizer for sugarcane in India?

Sugarcane requires a high amount of nitrogen fertilizer for proper growth. A 90:45:45 NPK fertilizer is often recommended for sugarcane in India.
